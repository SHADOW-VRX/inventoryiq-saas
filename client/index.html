Features
Everything you need to run your shop
From barcode scanning to real‑time analytics, InventoryIQ gives you the tools to manage your inventory like a pro.
Barcode Scanner
Scan any product barcode with your phone camera and instantly pull up stock info, price, and actions.
Real‑time Analytics
Track sales trends, inventory value, and stock health across your entire catalog with live dashboards.
Smart Alerts
Never run out. Get instant low‑stock and out‑of‑stock alerts with visual indicators and notifications.
Mobile First
Fully responsive design optimized for phones. Manage your inventory on any device, anywhere, anytime.
Multi‑Shop Ready
Each shop gets its own isolated data, settings, and subscription plan. Perfect for franchises.
Activity Log
Full audit trail of every sale, restock, product change, and login. Never lose track of changes.
